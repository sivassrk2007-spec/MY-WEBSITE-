Digital Infrastructure

Meta Ads, Google Ads, Landing Pages, GA4, Attribution

Digital marketing playbook covers complete paid + organic digital strategy for real estate. Includes: Meta campaign architecture, Google Ads account structure, landing page conversion principles, UTM taxonomy, GA4 event tracking, attribution modeling, automation flows, retargeting ladder. This is operational guide for daily digital execution.

Module 01
Meta Ads Campaign Architecture
Facebook + Instagram campaign structure for real estate lead generation. 3-tier funnel: Awareness → Consideration → Conversion.
  • Awareness campaigns: Video views, engagement, reach (cold audiences)
  • Consideration campaigns: Traffic, lead generation (warm audiences, remarketing)
  • Conversion campaigns: Conversions, catalog sales (hot audiences, site visitors)
  • Budget allocation: 30% awareness, 40% consideration, 30% conversion
  • Creative rotation: 3-5 creatives per ad set, refresh every 14 days
  • Audience segments: Lookalike (1-3%), interest-based, geo-targeted (5km radius)
  • Landing page: Project-specific (not homepage), mobile-optimized, load <3 seconds
Module 02
Google Ads Account Structure
Search + Display + YouTube campaign architecture. Keyword strategy aligned with buyer journey stages.
  • Search campaigns: High-intent keywords (location + property type + price)
  • Display campaigns: Remarketing to website visitors, contextual targeting
  • YouTube campaigns: In-stream video ads (awareness), bumper ads (remarketing)
  • Keyword structure: Exact match (bottom funnel), phrase match (mid), broad match modifier (top)
  • Negative keywords: Generic terms, competitor brands, unqualified searches
  • Quality Score optimization: Landing page relevance, ad copy alignment, expected CTR
  • Budget: 60% Search, 25% Display, 15% YouTube
Module 03
Landing Page Conversion Framework
Project-specific landing pages optimized for lead capture. Follows AIDA framework (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action).
  • Above fold: Hero image/video + headline + subheadline + single CTA
  • Trust signals: RERA registration, bank partnerships, delivery track record
  • Social proof: Testimonials, site visit photos, booking counter
  • Floor plans: Interactive, filterable by BHK/budget
  • Lead form: Name + Mobile + Email (3 fields maximum, progressive profiling)
  • Privacy: DPDP Act 2023 compliant consent checkbox
  • Load speed: <3 seconds on 4G, lazy loading for images below fold
Module 04
UTM Taxonomy & Tracking
Standardized UTM parameter structure for campaign attribution. Enables source/medium/campaign level reporting.
  • utm_source: facebook, google, instagram, youtube, whatsapp
  • utm_medium: cpc, cpm, display, video, social, email
  • utm_campaign: project-name_objective_month (e.g., vaanya_leads_jan2026)
  • utm_content: creative-variant (e.g., hero-video-1, carousel-floorplan)
  • utm_term: keyword (for paid search only)
  • Naming convention: lowercase, hyphens not underscores, max 50 characters
  • Tool: Campaign URL Builder spreadsheet for team (prevents inconsistencies)
Module 05
GA4 Event Tracking Setup
Google Analytics 4 configuration for real estate conversion tracking. Custom events for lead funnel.
  • Standard events: page_view, session_start, first_visit, user_engagement
  • Custom events: floor_plan_viewed, site_visit_requested, brochure_downloaded, video_played, form_submitted
  • Conversion events: lead_submitted, site_visit_booked, callback_requested
  • Ecommerce tracking: Not applicable (no online payment)
  • User properties: project_interest, budget_range, bhk_preference
  • GTM setup: All events fire via Google Tag Manager (not hardcoded)
  • Data retention: 14 months (maximum for free tier)
Module 06
Attribution Model Framework
Multi-touch attribution to understand full buyer journey. Assigns credit to each touchpoint.
  • Models supported: Last-click (default), First-click, Linear, Time-decay, Position-based
  • Real estate recommendation: Position-based (40% first touch, 40% last touch, 20% middle)
  • Rationale: First touch creates awareness, last touch drives conversion, middle nurtures
  • Tool: GA4 Model Comparison report + Data Studio dashboard
  • Limitation: Cannot track offline touchpoints (site hoarding, print ads)
  • Workaround: Unique phone numbers per offline channel (call tracking)
  • Review cadence: Monthly attribution analysis, quarterly model adjustment
Module 07
Marketing Automation Flows
Email + WhatsApp drip campaigns for lead nurturing. Triggered by user behavior.
  • Welcome series: 3 emails over 7 days (project intro, floor plans, testimonials)
  • Abandonment flow: Form started but not submitted → Follow-up in 2 hours
  • Nurture sequence: Weekly content (market insights, construction updates, resident stories)
  • Re-engagement: Inactive leads (30 days no activity) → Special offer/site visit invite
  • WhatsApp automation: Instant lead acknowledgment, appointment confirmation, reminder
  • Tool: HubSpot (mid-segment+) or Zoho (affordable segment)
  • Compliance: Unsubscribe link in every email, WhatsApp opt-out option
Module 08
Retargeting Ladder Strategy
Progressive retargeting based on engagement depth. Matches creative to buyer journey stage.
  • Tier 1: Website visitors (not engaged) → Generic awareness ads (brand story, amenities)
  • Tier 2: Engaged visitors (2+ pages, 60+ seconds) → Specific project benefits (location, specs)
  • Tier 3: High-intent (floor plan viewed, brochure downloaded) → Urgency messaging (inventory, offer deadline)
  • Tier 4: Form abandoners → Direct CTA (Complete registration, get callback)
  • Frequency cap: 3 impressions per week per user (prevents ad fatigue)
  • Exclusions: Converted leads, job seekers, competitors
  • Platforms: Meta retargeting, Google Display remarketing, YouTube sequential messaging
